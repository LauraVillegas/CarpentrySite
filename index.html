ABOUT US


I was born in Cuba and came to the United States for a better life. I am grateful for the opportunities I have been given as a legal immigrant and now US citizen. I am a diligent, hard working person who takes great pride in my work, attention to detail and exceptional customer service.

Currently, I work as a building superintendent at a luxury high-rise in downtown Palm Beach. The clientele in my building have excellent taste and expect high quality work which is exactly what I enjoy doing.


I created JG Carpentry Services, LLC because of the overwhelming demand I received from clients and their friends. I now have a flourishing Carpentry and repair business growing through word-of-mouth recommendations and repeated business.

My special skills include all aspects of carpentry, plumbing, bathroom and kitchen redesign and installation, HVAC repairs and maintenance, and appliance repair.
